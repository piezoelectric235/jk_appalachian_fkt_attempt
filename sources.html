Disclaimers

Karel: The GPX file from the FKT site has broken timestamps. After correction, I calculated less than 1800 miles - clearly inaccurate. I manually analyzed the GPX track to determine daily start and stop times, cross-checking with the official distance table and Karel's Strava activity logs. The resulting moving/rest times are only rough estimates, resulting in my total calculated time being 41d1h rather than the official 41d7h39m.

John: Strava GPX files lack timestamps. Instead, I extracted lap data manually. His pace may be inflated if pause times aren't reflected in laps, though moving and elapsed times are mostly consistent. Rest time is calculated as: next start time – previous elapsed time. Times don't match the official tracker splits, likely due to GPS errors. As of day 6, my ~50-mile lead estimate aligns with the live map measurement.

Tara: The GPX from the FKT page is missing Aug 14–20. I used the table screenshot instead. Since the GPX data and table disagree even on the first two days, I assume the GPX underreports distance and chose the table as the more complete source.
